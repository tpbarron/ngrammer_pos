<p>
<s>former paypal employees stephanie tilenius and osama bedier seen here at this weeks google wallet launch are at the center of a lawsuit alleging tradesecret theft</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  paypal and its parent company ebay sued google this week</s>
<s>the company claims that two of its former top executives violated their contracts by sharing confidential information with google and by negotiating with google despite a clear conflict of interest</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>california courts are famously unfriendly to employee noncompete agreements  which means ebay will have a hard fight to prove its case</s>
<s>heres a timeline of what ebay says happened based on the details provided in its lawsuit</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>march 12 2001 stephanie tilenius joins ebays staff and signs the companys nondisclosure agreement nda agreeing not to solicit any ebay ebay fortune 500 employees for one year after leaving the company and not to disclose confidential ebay information</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>nov 13 2002 osama bedier begins work at paypal and signs an nda</s>
<s>he eventually becomes a vice president in charge of bringing mobile payment technology and services to retailers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>january 2004 tilenius becomes vice president of paypal merchant services</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>2006 google goog fortune 500 launches paypal competitor google checkout</s>
<s>paypal researches the rival and briefs bedier on its weaknesses</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>2008 ebay begins negotiations with google to make paypal a payment option for mobile application purchases on the android market</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>oct 16 2009 tilenius resigns as ebays vice president of north america and global products and she reaffirms the nda she signed in 2001</s>
<s>though she stops working for ebay on a full time basis she stays on as a consultant for the company</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>2010 bedier becomes the senior paypal executive in the negotiations with google</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>feb 16 2010 google hires tilenius as vice president of electronic commerce</s>
<s>she is still consulting for ebay at the time</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>march 3 2010 tilenius stops consulting for ebay</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>june 2010 google names tilenius head of its new commerce and payments division</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>july 15 2010 tilenius begins attempting to lure bedier away from paypal to work for her at google</s>
<s>she writes a facebook message to bedier that says i heard from a little birdie that you might be open to bigger and better challenges i have a huge opportunity for you would love to chat if you are interested</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>aug 25 2010 tilenius asks bedier in a facebook message if he wants to speak with google senior vice president jeff huber</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>aug 27 2010 tilenius makes the connection between bedier and huber in an email saying you two should talk about the opportunity at google</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>september 2010 bedier begins interview process with google as he continues to negotiate with google on paypals behalf to make paypal a mobile payments option on the android market</s>
<s>ebay says bedier never informed the company of this conflict of interest</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>oct 21 2010 bedier culminates interview process with google by meeting with top google executives including thenpresident larry page</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>oct 26 2010 paypal and google finalize the android market deal</s>
<s>google however declines to sign the deal</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>oct 28 2010 in his paypal capacity bedier meets with android head andy rubin about googles strategic shift</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>oct 31 2010 google offers bedier a job</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>nov 1 2010 bedier informs paypal of his job offer</s>
<s>paypal tells bedier that he would be in violation of his contract if he accepted googles offer</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>nov 2 2010 bedier meets again with rubin to discuss how he could help google compete against paypal</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>nov 12 2010 google sends bedier a formal offer of employment but he does not immediately accept it</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>nov 19 2010 bedier asks tilenius whether he could bring paypal coworkers over to google with him in an email saying if folks want to join me on day one  how possible is this</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>nov 27 2010 tilenius sends bedier a text message saying by next week there will be more information i can share and you will see how the role at google could be expanded considerably</s>
<s> page speaks with bedier about expanding his proposed role at google and bedier formally accepts the job offer</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dec 2 2010 bedier changes his mind about google and informs paypal of his decision to remain at the company rather than accepting googles offer</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dec 3 2010 tilenius texts bedier asking if more money would help get him to change his mind</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>january 2011 bedier transfers paypals mobile payment strategy to his personal nonpaypal computer</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>jan 24 2011 bedier leaves paypal for google</s>
<s>paypal says bedier and google have misappropriated paypal trade secrets by disclosing them within google and to major retailers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>february 2011 google inks a revised agreement with paypal but google delays telling paypal that it signed the agreement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>april 7 2011 google reneges on paypal agreement informing the company that it plans on building a competing product</s>
<s>google recruits usman abbasi a highranking paypal engineer involved with mobile payments</s>
<s>may 26 2011 google launches its new google wallet mobilepayment offering</s>
<s>ebay files its lawsuit against google tilenius and bedier in californias state court in santa clara</s>
<s>google issued a statement friday saying it will fight the suit</s>
</p>
