<s>spanish treasury minister cristobal montoro shown here during a budget presentation in march said the credit market is seizing up for spain</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  spains treasury minister appealed to european leaders for financial support tuesday saying the countrys credit markets are seizing up</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the yield on spains 10year bond has been flirting dangerously close to the 7 mark that smacks of default anxiety</s>
<s>over the past week the 10year yield has been at its highest level since november</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>treasury minister cristobal montoro told spanish radio station onda cero that it is technically impossible for spain to bail itself out</s>
<s>he said that spain needs to get more money to improve its debt situation to open the bond markets back up so people can invest in the country</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the risk premium says spain doesnt have the market door open said montoro</s>
<s>the risk premium says that as a state we have a problem in accessing markets when we need to refinance our debt</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>economists estimate that the spanish government has about €800 billion in outstanding debt</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>meanwhile the european stability mechanism a bailout fund that comes into effect this summer will be equipped with only €500 billion</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>montoro said that spain wants to continue being part of the eurozone and must bet on the european institutions to financially support the fiscally troubled iberian nation</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the extent of spains fiscal troubles were unveiled in may when the government announced a €19 billion rescue of bankia one of spains top lenders</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>last week credit rating agency egan jones cut spains credit rating to b from bb and bank of spain governor miguel angel fernandez ordonez said he was resigning from his post a month early</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>montoro said that spanish banking crisis doesnt just involve his own country its a european problem</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>whats at stake is the euro and they must convince themselves and the markets that the euro is a reality and it does have a future he said</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the ministers appeal comes as leaders from the group of seven nations met to discuss europes debt crisis</s>
<s>the g7 ministers and leaders agreed to keep close tabs on the situation ahead of the larger g20 meeting later this month according to a us treasury statement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the g7 comments may be helping prop up spanish banking stocks which have taken a pretty harsh beating this year</s>
<s>shares of banco santander std and bbva bbva both rose about 15 tuesday</s>
<s>and the yield on spains 10year bond eased to 63</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>spains bond market will be tested thursday when the government is due to auction up to €2 billion worth of 2year 4year and 10year bonds</s>
</p>
