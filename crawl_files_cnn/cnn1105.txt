</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  minnesota residents are far from pleased about their states government shutdown</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>for some it means no fun in sun during the warm holiday weekend since the state parks are closed</s>
<s>for others it means no job since more than 20000 state employees were laid off</s>
<s>and for still others it means no safety net since caseworkers arent answering their phones</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>all but essential state government services ceased on friday after governor mark dayton and republican lawmakers failed to resolve their budget dispute before the new fiscal year began</s>
<s>a $14 billion gap separates them</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the governor wants to close the shortfall by raising taxes on the wealthiest 19 of minnesotans</s>
<s>republicans want to limit spending</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>but its minnesotans like virginia becker who are feeling the impact</s>
<s>the minnetonka resident wont be able to continue to her twiceweekly poker game at canterbury park race track</s>
<s>the shutdown means thousands of people are out of a job or out of luck if they wanted to go to the track during the busy holiday weekend she said</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>its a beautiful facility where thousands of people enjoy the short summer in minnesota and now its closed becker said</s>
<s>its the worst weekend it could have happened</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the state shutdown also ruined beckers daughters plans</s>
<s>she was supposed to camp in one of minnesotas parks over the weekend but will now stay home since the parks are all closed</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>for cherise payton the shutdown could have been much worse</s>
<s>the single mother who was recently homeless feared that she would not receive state aid to pay her rent</s>
<s>she learned friday that she would get her check over the weekend but shes still on edge because she cannot reach her state caseworkers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>if a crisis comes up who do i call</s>
<s> said payton who lives in duluth with her three teenagers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>homeless prevention agencies are among those scrambling to continue providing for their clients</s>
<s>simpson housing services will lose state funding for a variety of programs if a judge does not approve its appeal to be deemed essential</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>without the state grants the minneapolis agency will have to scale back the support services it provides its clients said julie manworren simpsons executive director</s>
<s>since they have to focus mainly on basic needs the staff will not be able to assist clients with job searches and mental health issues</s>
<s>if the standoff continues into august 12 families in transitional housing will find themselves back on the streets</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>families who saw that they were turning the corner and ready to have stability are now thrust back into terror manworren said</s>
<s>everything theyve been working for is about to crumble</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the shutdown shuttered many state agencies and nonprofit organizations that cater to the needy though some social service programs kept their funding</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a judge ordered wednesday that the state continue to support programs that are paid for with federal dollars as well as maintain benefit payments and medical services</s>
<s>several nonprofits appealed to the court to also be considered critical</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the minnesota zoo was set to reopen sunday after the judge ruled that it could use funds from admissions parking concessions memberships and donations to operate</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>kay kuppe however is not one of the fortunate ones</s>
<s>a state worker for 10 years kuppe joins her husband on the unemployment line</s>
<s>while she can apply for jobless benefits next week kuppe is concerned about how the couple will manage financially if the standoff continues for weeks</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the fridley resident has stopped shopping and going to dinner and the movies</s>
<s>she halted her automatic mortgage payments and called her bank to inquire about deferring a few payments</s>
<s>she is also looking to apply to any city jobs in case the stalemate isnt resolved soon</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>but most of all kuppe wants to return to her job as a business analyst in the department of human services</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>someone has to say uncle she said referring to the elected officials</s>
<s>how long can this go on</s>
</p>
