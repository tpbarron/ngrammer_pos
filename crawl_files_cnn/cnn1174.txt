<p>
<s>in a widely expected move the ecb cut rates to an alltime low</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  the european central bank lowered interest rates thursday to alltime lows but did not introduce any unconventional moves to stimulate economic activity</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the ecb cut its main lending rate by a quarter of a percentage point to 075</s>
<s>the move was widely expected by the market and followed a surprise rate cut announcement by chinas central bank earlier thursday</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>during a press conference ecb president mario draghi said the banks decision was unanimous</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in addition to cutting its main lending rate the ecb also lowered two other key interest rates</s>
<s>the interest rate on a marginal lending facility was decreased to 15 and interest rates on deposits were lowered to zero</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>some investors had been hoping the ecb would cut rates to 05</s>
<s>during his press conference draghi declined to discuss what other steps the bank might take</s>
<s>he did stress that the ecb has more tools at its disposal if the economy should continue to falter</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>theres not such a feeling that were running low on policy options said draghi</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>separately the bank of england held rates steady thursday morning at 05</s>
<s>but the boe agreed to back an additional £50 billion in asset purchases raising its total to £375 billion</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>investors had been hoping for coordinated action by central banks to prop up the faltering global economy</s>
<s>while thursdays moves at first blush appeared to be another step in that direction investors didnt think they did enough</s>
<s>european and us markets sold off following draghis press conference</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>these moves were highly necessary and widely expected but are insufficient to turn the eurozone economy around said holger schmieding chief economist at berenberg bank</s>
<s>the ecb would have to do more to intervene to calm market anxiety</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>draghi offered sober assessments of the eurozones economic growth prospects throughout his press conference</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>we see now weakening of growth in the whole of the euro area including countries that had not experienced that before he said</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>while draghi did not give any indication that the ecb might consider a third longterm refinancing operation to funnel funds into european banks he said that the socalled ltro did help reverse outflows of capital from the eurozone</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>but the two previous ltro programs were unable to increase lending around the eurozone draghi added noting that the ecb cannot manufacture demand for loans</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>last week euro area political leaders agreed to move towards a centralized banking authority under the direction of the ecb</s>
<s>yields on spanish and italian bonds have eased since last week</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>but economic conditions in the euro area have deteriorated with declines in manufacturing activity pointing to a sharp slowdown in the second quarter</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>at the same time inflation in the eurozone was unchanged in june at 24 while prices fell in germany and spain</s>
<s>the ecbs target rate for inflation is 2 but the central bank has said it expects prices to remain anchored in the nearterm</s>
</p>
