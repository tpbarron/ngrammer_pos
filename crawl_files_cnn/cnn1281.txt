<s>when remodeling create a financial plan upfront and contractors can tailor proposals to fit what you can afford</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>money magazine  budgeting for a large remodeling project presents a bit of a chickenandegg problem you wont have a feel for the cost until you get bids from contractors</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>but unless you give pros a ballpark figure from the start theyll have to guess at what to include in their bids  and theyll come back to you with a huge range of prices for very different plans</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a faucet can cost $200 to $900 a window can be $400 to $1200 says madison contractor mike gasch</s>
<s>i need to know where to aim</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>to solve this conundrum do some calculations first</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>1</s>
<s>start with average costs </s>
</p>

<p>
<s>when insurance companies need to pinpoint construction costs they multiply the length by the width of the space and then multiply that by the projects typical cost per square foot</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>albert paxton an estimator who provides such data to claims adjusters and contractors pegs average persquarefoot costs of remodeling jobs at this</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>2</s>
<s>tweak to fit the scope</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>these numbers are for gut remodels meaning the room is demolished right down to the framing and rebuilt</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>with a less involved project  in the kitchen say you might be refacing the cabinets and replacing the countertops and appliances instead of tearing out everything  cut your number by about 30 says paxton</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>for a cosmetic update as in fresh paint on the cabinets plus new lighting and hardware reduce it by about 60</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>3</s>
<s>adjust for your location</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a plumber working in manhattan might charge twice the hourly rate of one in statesboro ga</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>same goes for everyone from laborers to architects</s>
<s>in a metro area along the northeast or pacific seaboards add 30 to 40 use your judgment about your market</s>
<s>in a rural area especially in the south or midwest drop it by 15 to 20</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>4</s>
<s>modify for style</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>these figures assume youre buying midrange fixtures and finishes</s>
<s>if youre going upscale as in granite countertops and stainlesssteel appliances or stone bathroom tiles and a separate soaking tub and walkin shower raise your number by 30 to 40 depending on how elaborate youre getting</s>
<s>for an economy job say at a rental property cut it by 25</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>make your number fit your finances by adjusting items 2 and 4 then be upfront about your bottom line with contractors who look at the job</s>
</p>
