<p>
<s>can i open an account with a us broker to buy stocks and bonds that trade in other countries</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>—ric</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>yes</s>
<s>two brokerages that make it especially easy are fidelity and etrade</s>
<s>at fidelity for example you can trade on exchanges in 17 countries from belgium to singapore</s>
<s>buying local though has drawbacks</s>
<s>commissions in some places are high and you could also face currencyconversion fees of up to 1</s>
<s>foreign companies arent subject to us financial disclosure rules and local taxes on foreignpaid dividends can be a hassle</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a simpler alternative is to buy one of the 400 or so american depositary receipts that trade on us exchanges</s>
<s>adrs hold shares of foreign firms and you can buy and sell and collect dividends in dollars</s>
</p>
