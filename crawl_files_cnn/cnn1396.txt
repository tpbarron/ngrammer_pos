<p>
<s>hiring is picking up in several sectors led by manufacturers that are benefiting from a weak dollar and technology firms gearing up to meet growing demand from businesses upgrading their software and systems</s>
<s>health care remains a hiring stalwart and even nonprofits which were hit especially hard by funding cuts in 2009 and 2010 are expected to rebound</s>
</p>

<p>

<p>
<s>the action plan prove to your boss  or a new employer  that you have expertise thats in demand</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>fill the markets gaps</s>
<s>the shortage of skilled professional workers is a real opportunity</s>
<s>think youre lacking a key skill set</s>
<s>find out if theres a jobrotation program at your firm</s>
<s>and work toward certification or training in areas of need at your company</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>its not easy to find the time to invest if youre working or job hunting but that kind of initiative gives you a clear advantage says geoff hoffmann chief operating officer at executive recruiting firm dhr international</s>

<p>
<s>cover your gaps</s>
<s>if youre unemployed youll have a hard time landing jobs with big employment gaps on your résumé</s>

</p>

<p>
<s>raise your profile</s>
<s>you already know how valuable social media are for job hunting</s>
<s>but they can be used to land a promotion or a betterthanaverage salary bump as well</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>for example use linkedins new skills tool to peruse profiles of higherups and top performers at your firm to see what experience they highlight</s>
</p>

<s>dont know them personally</s>
