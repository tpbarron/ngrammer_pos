<p>
<s>a blockbuster merger between mining companies xstrata and glencore cleared a hurdle monday</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>hong kong cnnmoney  the prospects of a huge consolidation in the international mining industry looked brighter monday after xstratas board of directors said it was backing a revised offer from glencore international</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the prospective merger was first announced in february but talks had sputtered in the face of shareholder opposition</s>
<s>should the union proceed the resulting company would have a market capitalization of around $80 billion</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>under the terms of the deal xstrata shareholders would receive 305 glencore shares for each share they hold</s>
<s>the companies said the price marks a 176 premium over the value of xstrata shares on february 1 when talk of the deal first surfaced</s>
<s>shareholders will be asked to approve the merger in a vote</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the strategic rationale for combining xstrata and glencore remains highly compelling xstrata ceo mick davis said in a statement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>glencore went public last year and its stock is listed on londons ftse 100 and in hong kong</s>
<s>xstrata xsraf is traded on exchanges in london and zurich</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>xstrata is the worlds fourthlargest copper producer and one of the largest zinc and nickel miners</s>
<s>the company which employs 70000 people in 20 countries reported a 2011 net profit of $57 billion on nearly $34 billion in revenue</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>by comparison glencore employs nearly 60000 people in at least 40 countries with interests in a variety of metals and minerals as well as oil coal and agricultural commodities such as wheat corn oil seeds and sugar</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related glencore xstrata tieup would create giant</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>under the new terms of the deal glencore chief executive ivan glasenberg will take control of the company six months after the merger is complete</s>
<s>that differs from the original offer  in which xstratas davis would have remained at the companys helm</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the new deal also includes monetary incentives designed to keep xstratas team of mining executives with the new company  something that had reportedly upset select shareholders</s>
</p>
