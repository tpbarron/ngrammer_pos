<p>

</p>

<p>
<s>microsoft msft fortune 500 avoided paying nearly $7 billion by transferring almost half of its us revenue to a subsidiary in puerto rico and moving patents to foreign subsidiaries</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>hewlettpackard hpq fortune 500 also dodged taxes  although the report didnt indicate how much  by creating subsidiaries abroad and making loans to itself</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>small business owners say they cant offshore profits and take advantage of these opportunities</s>
<s>the strategies require a worldwide presence and are either too complex or too costly</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>but those business owners say thats not the only issue</s>
<s>theyre bothered by the effect of depleting the governments revenue stream which creates pressure to cut government spending that the nations 27 million businesses rely on</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>this tax money goes to support the infrastructure that allows our businesses to be successful said joseph rotella owner of spencer organ an instrument repair company in waltham massachusetts</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in 2010 spencer organ paid $47000 in taxes while microsoft gave $44 billion</s>
<s>still they paid nearly the same effective federal tax rate of 25</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>these big companies avoid paying their fair share rotella said noting that highly profitable firms rarely pay the actual top federal rate of 35</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>us powerhouses are defending their offshoring of profits by claiming the us corporate tax rate is too high</s>
<s>currently a corporation is taxed abroad at the foreign countrys rate and if the united states tax rate is higher profits heading back to the united states are taxed the difference</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>to hotel owner sue edgington whose adventure inn is located deep in the woods of northeast minnesota the issue of paying taxes is one of patriotism</s>
<s>like most small business owners shes fiscally conservative and doesnt gladly fork over more in taxes</s>
<s>however she said that when companies avoiding paying them it threatens funding to public colleges like the one she attended  and protection of wildlife like the kind that draws tourists her way</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>it angers me she said</s>
<s>its morally wrong</s>
<s>that money is being pulled out of our economy</s>
<s>theres a moral obligation to keep it here because they live in this country and have been able to take advantage of that</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>her frustration could be directed at several of the nations top companies</s>
