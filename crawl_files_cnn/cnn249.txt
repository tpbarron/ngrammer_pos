<p>
<s>theres more than one way to cash in on corporate americas latest craze the spinoff</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>this article was published in the november issue of money magazine</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>by paul r la monica</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>youd think in a sluggish economy corporations sitting on mountains of cash would try to use some of that money to buy growth through acquisitions</s>
<s>lately though the opposite has been taking place</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>kraft krft just completed the sale of its global snack business to the public through a spinoff</s>
<s>other giants like news corp nwsa pfizer pfe and safeway swy have also announced plans to streamline by splintering off unwanted subsidiaries</s>
<s>joe cornell president of research firm spinoff advisors says more than 80 companies have announced or completed splits since 2011  double the pace from 2009â€“10</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>why the rush to break up</s>
<s>in a slowgrowing world firms are racing to make their operations more efficient</s>
<s>execs also realize that pureplay firms are more easily understood and are being rewarded with higher valuations cornell says</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>so whats the smartest way to invest in this downsizing trend</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>think like a value investor</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>go with the spinoff</s>
<s>over long periods beatendown underappreciated stocks generally outpace the market and whats more unloved than a business unit that gets kicked to the curb</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>once freed newly independent companies have more incentive to grow and more control over their capital says john carey manager of the pioneer fund</s>
<s>no wonder spinoffs have risen 180 since march 2009 vs 120 for the sp</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>of course there are risks many spinoffs start off as small enterprises and some are jettisoned for good reason</s>
<s>you can reduce that risk by going with a diversified fund</s>
<s>the guggenheim spinoff index etf csd which invests in 24 small and midsize spinoffs has beaten the sp 500 by more than four percentage points a year over the past three years mimicking a longerterm trend</s>
<s>among this etfs holdings are cablevision cvc spin off madison square garden msg and exexpedia unit tripadvisor trip</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>play both sides</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in some spinoffs the sum of the parts is worth more than the whole says gerry sparrow of sparrow capital management</s>
<s>so it can pay to hold both parent and progeny</s>
<s>he owns mcdonalds mcd and its former unit chipotle mexican grill cmg</s>
<s>both have whipped the sp 500 over the past five years</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>among upcoming spinoffs cornell likes mcgrawhill mhp pushed by shareholders to split off its textbook business mcgrawhill education</s>
<s>he thinks the move will allow the firms financialservices information unit which includes the standard  poors rating agency to be more focused</s>
<s>and education becomes the secondbiggest player in the textbook biz</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>pioneers carey owns pfizer pfe which is splitting off its underappreciated animal health unit</s>
<s>zoetis set to be the biggest veterinary drug company saw its sales rise 18 last year</s>
<s>the move will allow pfizer to focus all its energy on developing blockbuster human drugs</s>
<s>in other words winwin</s>
</p>
