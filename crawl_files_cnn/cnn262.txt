<p>
<s>the doj gave the green light to a controversial deal between verizon and several of the nations largest cable providers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  the us department of justice said thursday that it will approve a controversial spectrum sale deal between verizon comcast and three other cable companies after some revisions to remove terms that the doj considers anticompetitive</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>verizon vz fortune 500 agreed late last year to buy $36 billion of unused wireless spectrum held by the nations four largest cable providers comcast cmcsatime warner cable twc fortune 500 cox communications and bright house networks</s>
<s>verizon wants the spectrum to help build out its 4g network</s>
<s>in return the cable companies will be able to buy access to verizons 4g services and bundle it with their own tripleplay offerings</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the companies plan to crosssell each others services</s>
<s>verizon will advertise and bundle cable services with its own wireless and landline services and the cable companies can do the same for verizon  but not for verizons competitors</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>that raised red flags with the doj</s>
<s>the agency is concerned because verizons fios cable television and broadband internet service is a major rival to the cable companies offerings</s>
<s>verizon and the cable companies are direct competitors and the proposed deal would have threatened this competition according to the doj</s>
<s>verizons incentive to aggressively sell its own services would have been reduced the doj said</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>as a result the justice department filed an antitrust lawsuit against the companies  one all the parties agreed to immediately settle following promises to meet certain conditions</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related story verizons $36 billion spectrum buy reshapes wireless field</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the agreement bars verizon from selling cable services in areas where fios is available</s>
<s>the agency also limited the length of the agreement to five years ending in december 2016</s>
<s>at that point the cable companies can begin selling other wireless companies services</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the doj had a few additional demands</s>
<s>it said verizon must retain the ability to bundle its dsl internet services with satellite tv providers</s>
<s>the company cut its partnership with directv in december though verizon never explicitly said that move was a result of its proposed deal with the cable providers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the department has provided the right remedy for competition and consumers joseph wayland head of the department of justices antitrust division said in a prepared statement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the deal is still contingent upon the federal communications commissions approval which the agency is likely to grant soon</s>
<s>julius genachowski the regulators chairman said thursday that he will urge the fccs other commissioners to approve the deal following the companies binding procompetitive commitments with the doj</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the deals two largest partners comcast and verizon said they were pleased that the arrangement cleared an important hurdle</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>we are pleased that the consent decree  preserves the most important goals of the agreements david cohen comcasts executive vice president said in a statement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>separately the doj said it would allow verizon to sell some of its unwanted spectrum to tmobile</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>that deal had come under fire by some consumer advocates who viewed the simultaneous selling and buying of spectrum as a sign that verizon didnt need to complete the deal with the cable companies</s>
<s>but the doj approved the tmobile deal saying it will benefit tmobiles customers</s>
</p>
