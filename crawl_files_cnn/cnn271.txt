<p>
<s>megaupload founder kim dotcom awaiting trial on various criminal charges says he is working on a new business venture</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  just one week after being released on bail in new zealand kim dotcom the badboy german founder of notorious cyberlocker megaupload announced on twitter that he is planning to launch a music service called megabox later this year</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>megabox has been on the drawing board for a while dotcom first mentioned it in a guest post for torrent freak the month before his arrest</s>
<s>the venture will allow artists to sell their creations direct to consumers and allowing sic artists to keep 90 of earnings he wrote</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dotcoms legal problems apparently havent dented his entrepreneurial zeal</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dotcom made a fortune as the founder and longtime ceo of megaupload a filesharing site that became one of the internets largest bastions of pirated content</s>
<s>it was shut down in january by us federal agents when dotcom and seven other megaupload officers were indicted for criminal copyright infringement and other crimes</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dotcom is a permanent resident of new zealand and faces extradition</s>
<s>his hearing was originally slated for august 6 but was postponed to march 2013 after the high court judge overseeing his case ruled that the warrants used to seize dotcoms property in an earlymorning raid were illegal</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>that judge later recused himself from the case after his remarks at a conference on copyright law indicated that he might be less than impartial</s>
<s>he called the us the enemy for its attempt to enforce copyright internationally</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dotcom and his associates are now free on bail</s>
<s>the flamboyant founder  who prosecutors say made more than $42 million from megaupload in 2010 alone  lost no time in getting back to work</s>
<s>on his twitter feed he has been touting his plans to create a service catering to artists who are eager to depart from outdated business models</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dotcom and his us lawyers did not respond to cnnmoneys requests for comment</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related story megaupload and the twilight of copyright</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>if megabox launches it will be entering an increasingly crowded space for musicfocused tech startups</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>streaming music service spotify which brought its service to the us last year is one of the industrys most closely watched ventures</s>
<s>dozens of smaller startups are taking aim at other aspects of the music market</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>megabox could find itself going headtohead with san franciscobased gumroad</s>
<s>the nofrills site allows creators to sell almost anything directly to the public  and its starting to catch on with musicians</s>
<s>rapper wiz khalifa has begun using the platform gumroad business development head adam besvinick announced two weeks ago on his blog</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>another venture gaining steam is fan management service tracksby started in 2011 by former ustream staffers matt schlicht and mazyar mazy kazerooni</s>
<s>the platform lets artists build social media features directly into the music and videos they release</s>
<s>a tracksby campaign for rapper lil wayne last year garnered more than 100000 email addresses from fans in its first day</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>even as he readies his new megabox venture dotcom is fighting to revive his previous business</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>by us court order megauploads website remains offline while legal proceedings are underway but the case isnt cutanddried</s>
<s>dotcoms alleged crimes may not be extraditable under new zealands treaty with the us fortune magazine writer roger parloff pointed out in a recent story on the cases complexity  and they may not even be crimes</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>megaupload claims that it was in compliance with the terms of the digital millennium copyright act and its throwing a ton of legal firepower at proving the point</s>
<s>lead attorney ira rothken who is also representing dotcom personally says the company has a team of 25 lawyers working on its behalf across the globe</s>
</p>
