<p>
<s>walmart is cutting the fee it charges layaway customers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  walmart stores said tuesday it is cutting the upfront fee it charges customers who use its layaway plan</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>walmart wmt fortune 500 the nations largest retailer announced the change in fee  going from $15 to $5  soon after retailer toys r us announced it would offer its customers free layaway on purchases made by oct 31</s>
<s>after that date toys r us will also charge $5</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>layaway allows customers to hold an item for a period of time while they make incremental payments to cover its total cost</s>
<s>walmart had stopped allowing layaway purchases after the 2006 holiday season but brought the practice back last year</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>walmart said its customers will get the upfront fee back on a walmart gift card once they complete payments on the items they had on layaway</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>we believe this rollback strengthens our layaway offering said duncan mac naughton chief merchandising and marketing officer for walmarts us unit</s>
<s>its even more attractive to our customers and makes walmart more competitive in the marketplace</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>walmart reported improved sales and earnings for its just completed quarter and raised its financial targets for the rest of the fiscal year</s>
<s>still it is facing generally sluggish retail sales along with tough competition from retailers such as target tgt fortune 500</s>
<s>all retailers are striving to attract holiday shoppers despite a relatively weak economy and high unemployment</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>layaway has become an increasingly popular method to make purchases</s>
<s>earlier this summer sears shld fortune 500 announced that it would offer vacation packages on layaway through its searsvacationscom site</s>
</p>
