<p>
<s>ready to get started</s>
<s>here are the forms you need to fill out  and the calls you need to make  to apply for needbased grants and other aid</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>step 1</s>
<s>fill out the free application for federal student aid</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>accepted by almost every accredited college the fafsa is the single most important financial aid application youll need to fill out</s>
<s>it will be used to determine whether you are eligible for both federal needbased grants such as pell grants as well as many states grants</s>
<s>in addition the fafsa also qualifies you for federal college loans and many charities and colleges also base their scholarship decisions on it</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>since some colleges dole out aid on a firstcome firstserved basis you should complete your fafsa form as soon after january 1 as possible</s>
<s>but dont panic if youre late</s>
<s>you can apply for retroactive aid as late as nine months after the fact</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>each academic years fafsa is available for 18 months</s>
<s>if you get this form from the government and fill it out yourself its free</s>
<s>if you choose to get help from a consultant or a private business such as fafsacom expect to pay a fee  much the same way youd pay an accountant to fill out a free tax form</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>when using the online form the student and a parent if the student is under 24 have to apply online for fafsa personal identification numbers</s>
<s>after you have a fafsa identification number you then provide information about the students savings and previous years earnings</s>
<s>most students under the age of 24 must also provide information about their parents savings and earnings</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>it helps  but is not necessary  to have completed your tax form before you start your fafsa</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>step 2</s>
<s>fill out the college boards financial aid profile</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>check if your college or scholarship application requires the college boards cssfinancial aid profile</s>
<s>about 300 colleges mostly private schools require this additional and more indepth financial aid application as well</s>
<s>heres a list of the schools that require this additional form</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the college board charges $9 for the application and another $16 to send it to each college you designate</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>step 3</s>
<s>make some calls</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>call your colleges financial aid office and ask about additional opportunities for needbased aid</s>
</p>
