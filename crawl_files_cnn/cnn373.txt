<p>
<s>former alice cooper drummer neal smith is now in the real estate biz</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>fortune  neal smith the former drummer for the rock band alice cooper flipped his first house in the early 70s</s>
<s>the bands 1972 megahit schools out had just been released and its members were flush with cash says smith now 65</s>
<s>so he used some of the proceeds to buy property in phoenix</s>
<s>two years later he sold it tripling his investment</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>smith had caught the real estate bug but he wouldnt pursue it as a career for more than a decade</s>
<s>smith had joined alice cooper in 1967 a year after he met alice vincent furnier and the other band members at a community college in arizona</s>
<s>they moved to los angeles and came out with two albums neither of which was successful</s>
<s>then in 1971 the band broke through with its single im eighteen</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>more second acts how a bra seller found her fit</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>alice cooper was known for its stage antics and its use of props such as decapitated dolls and electric chairs</s>
<s>the band wanted to live in westchester county ny but their accountant told them to move to connecticut for the lower taxes</s>
<s>they ended up in a mansion in greenwich where they used to do fullblown shows in the ballroom recalls smith</s>
<s>he loved the area</s>
<s>i could drive down greenwich in my rolls and nobody cared because their rollsroyces were bigger and newer he says</s>
<s>when alice cooper broke up in the mid70s smith decided to stay in connecticut</s>
<s>in 1985 his then girlfriend suggested that he enroll in a real estate course with her</s>
<s>as soon as he procured his license his girlfriends mom a local realtor offered him a job</s>
<s>he sold his first property in less than two weeks</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>more second acts lacrosse by jake</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>smith started driving around fairfield county scouting out listings every day</s>
<s>sales came naturally to him</s>
<s>its like the music business  you learn the rudiments but when you start going on the road it has to come from inside of you he says</s>
<s>at first smith was reluctant to tout his rockandroll pedigree</s>
<s>but in the 1990s a friend persuaded him to market himself online using the name rock n realtor</s>
<s>since then a few fans have sought him out  but most clients dont know his past</s>
<s>today smith works for william pitt sothebys international realty selling highend properties in connecticut his biggest transaction involved a $5 million home</s>
<s>he hasnt given up on music</s>
<s>smith reunited with alice cooper last year for the bands induction into the rock and roll hall of fame he also has a solo act called killsmith</s>
<s>like alice cooper killsmith is a dark sinister character says smith</s>
<s>he just happens to sell houses on the side</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>advice for career changers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>follow your passion</s>
<s>a lot of people just suffer and go from one career to another says smith</s>
<s>find something that youre interested in and learn about it before you venture out</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>be patient</s>
<s>when you get into business dont necessarily think youre going to make money in the first year</s>
<s>youre building your sphere of influence</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>play the long game</s>
<s>in real estate the only thing someone cant take away from you is your reputation</s>
<s>you have to be graceful and you have to be honest</s>
</p>
