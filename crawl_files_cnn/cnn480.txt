<p>
<s>deborah kenny from president of sesame street publishing to founder and ceo of harlem village academies</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>fortune  before deborah kenny quit her job in 2001 to start a school for underprivileged youth in harlem her friends staged an intervention</s>
<s>they listed their concerns she would run out of money shed lose her house and worst of all as a new widow she wouldnt be able to adequately provide for her three young children</s>
<s>kenny did not budge</s>
<s>i felt like i would die if i didnt start this school she says</s>
<s>kenny found herself sketching out the plans for her new education program during meetings the way others might doodle</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>after she left her job she set up shop in a corner of the playroom in the basement of her house</s>
<s>funding nearly fell through her charter applications almost got denied and she even had to take out a bridge loan from her neighbor</s>
<s>still kenny did not drift from her vision</s>
<s>in 2003 buoyed by a $1 million gates foundation grant harlem village academies launched its first middle school and opened a second in 2005</s>
<s>to call the schools a wild success would be an understatement</s>
<s>in 2007 students posted such high test scores that president bush visited</s>
<s>hvas eighthgraders have hit 100 math proficiency for the past four years</s>
<s>attrition across all grades in 201112 was just 4 though kenny isnt shy about holding students back a grade</s>
<s>it added a high school in 2007 and this summer graduated its first class of seniors who went on to attend the likes of yale bard and occidental</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>kenny straddles two worlds  business and education</s>
<s>the windows of her office overlook blocks of housing projects and her bookshelf is packed with management guides peter druckers management challenges for the 21st century jack welchs jack straight from the gut</s>
<s>in a single breath she namechecks barry diller before citing toyotas management philosophy and rainer maria rilkes letters to a young poet</s>
<s>her title at hva is ceo</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>kennys founding philosophy is to create a teacherfocused institution</s>
<s>she has boosted instructor pay and gives them freedom to craft their own curriculum</s>
<s>remarkably hva students arent taught the test despite such strong test scores</s>
<s>she consulted with michael dell and welch on improving employee productivity and morale</s>
<s>a maniacal work ethic is the common practice at hva and kenny seems to lead the charge  the last time kenny took a vacation was four years ago</s>
<s>soon she plans to add two more elementary schools and a guidance center for hva alums</s>
<s>and her book about starting hva and her mission to empower teachers born to rise comes out in early june</s>
<s>when youre in the right line she says work is more fun than fun</s>
</p>
