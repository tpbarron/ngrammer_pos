<p>
<s>fortune  in november of 1977 jake steinfeld a freshman lacrosse player at the state university of new york at cortland was standing on a field in the hail</s>
<s>what am i doing</s>
<s> he wondered</s>
<s>it was freezing and he saw no future in the sport</s>
<s>so steinfeld  a weightlifting enthusiast since age 13  dropped out moved to northridge calif and became a bodybuilder</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>after winning a few small competitions he moved over to studio city and fell into a routine lifting weights in the morning working as a bouncer at night</s>
<s>sandra will an actress approached him one day and told him she needed someone to help her get in shape for a commercial</s>
<s>he trained her she told friends about his services and word spread</s>
<s>among many other big names steinfeld worked with steven spielberg  who later hired him to help harrison ford train to play indiana jones  and named his company body by jake</s>
<s>he offered personal training by appointment exercise videos and eventually tv spots</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>steinfeld milked his hollywood connections grew the business and in 1993 launched a 24hour network fittv with tim robertson son of televangelist pat</s>
<s>in 1998 steinfeld sold fittv to news corp nwsa fortune 500 for an undisclosed figure</s>
<s>he had money recognizable celebrity and an itch to try a new venture</s>
<s>his mind returned to those freezing lacrosse practices</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>he had read about dave morrow an allamerican lacrosse player who founded warrior a lacrosse equipment and apparel maker</s>
<s>on a whim steinfeld called morrow to persuade him to create a professional lacrosse league</s>
<s>no such thing existed</s>
<s>he just called me out of the blue morrow says</s>
<s>it was a 310 number so i thought it was one of my old teammates messing with me</s>
<s>when i called back someone answered and said this is craig from body by jake and i almost hung up the phone</s>
<s> morrow didnt and within minutes the gregarious body by jake guy had convinced him</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>more steal bases snag sponsors</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the two former laxers along with robertson funded major league lacrosse mll by themselves</s>
<s>but by the end of the first season in 2000 mll was out of money steinfeld says</s>
<s>the trio scrounged for years</s>
<s>a turning point came in 2004 morrow sold warrior to new balance and its owner jim davis bought a controlling stake in the league saving it</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>today the league has just wrapped up its 12th season with its championship match played on august 26 at harvard stadium in boston</s>
<s>while the mll is growing nicely many american sports fans still arent aware that pro lacrosse even exists</s>
<s>dave gross mll commissioner says thats to be expected  it seems that creating a sports league from scratch is not easy</s>
<s>we are not going to be an overnight success he says but nothing in sports happens that way</s>
<s>were not the nfl were not even the mls at this point</s>
<s>but were growing it in the right way</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>mike stone a midfielder for the boston cannons 2011 champions says recognition isnt why the players are in it anyway</s>
<s>right now with the state of the mll youre doing this because you love the sport he says</s>
<s>we all know nobody is making a killing at it and its a grind for everybody</s>
<s> indeed pros make only $13000 on average per season thus they all have regular jobs</s>
<s>the season is short  13 weeks  and falls in summer</s>
<s>still guys like stone get paid to do what they love all thanks to steinfeld whose name graces the playoffs trophy</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the body by jake boss is now focusing his time on beefing up the leagues size and reach he helped launch the program inside the mll which began airing this season on cbs sports network</s>
<s>he has people working on technology that will make it easier for a casual fan to watch the sport</s>
<s>tv footage will now highlight the player who has the ball much like in video games and akin to what fox tried to do with pro hockey when it highlighted the zooming puck in the late 90s</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the mll has sponsorship deals with beverage bigs like powerade coke zero and bud light</s>
<s>this year two expansion teams debuted the charlotte hounds and the ohio machine</s>
<s>the latter in a june game against the denver outlaws pulled a crowd of 30128 the biggest in mll history</s>
<s>and it will continue to expand the mll plans to add two more teams in florida texas or atlanta by 2014</s>
<s>after that its shooting for four new teams on the west coast which would bring the total to 14</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>were getting close steinfeld hopes to the tipping point</s>
<s> and hollywood still beckons lionsgate bought take a shot a book by steinfeld and morrow about creating the league with plans to make it into a tv drama series</s>
</p>
