<s>as i zig and zag i can spot a thirsty hydrangea a wasps nest or a loose porch screen</s>
<s>plus since i so often write about yard care its important to actually do yard care</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>witness these time  and money  saving tricks learned in the saturday morning sun</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>treat your gas</s>
<s>the secret to getting power equipment to start on the first pull isnt hauling it in for $50 to $90 tuneups</s>
<s>its all about the gas which begins to degrade as soon as you pump it</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>after a couple of months never mind a whole winter it will gum up the carburetor and you could shred a rotator cuff trying to start your mower</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>see also 4 ways to save on landscaping</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>theres a simple solution a few drops of fuel stabilizer</s>
<s>one $6 bottle of stabil has kept my tools purring like lions for two years and counting</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>dont bag clippings</s>
<s>every turf scientist and workaday landscaper ive questioned recommends forgoing the collection bag and setting the mower to mulch</s>
<s>that pulverizes the clippings and recycles them into the soil saving water and fertilizer costs</s>
<s>it also keeps me honest</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>with the bag i might get lazy and let the lawn reach meadow height before finally chopping it down bad news since removing more than a third of their height harms the plants but the mower cant effectively mulch that much material</s>
<s>and my better half wont abide the clumps of hay it leaves behind</s>
<s>so unless i want to rake i have to mow often</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>let the pros fertilize</s>
<s>passing joggers and dog walkers may think im some sort of grass whisperer but fertilizer is beyond me</s>
<s>i cant interpret the backofbag chart and i once applied a weedandfeed mix when the temperature was over 80Â° f and burned out the backyard</s>
<s>now i hire a crew to fertilize and aerate in the fall for around $600 a year</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>see also lowcost ways to put off pricey home repairs</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>edge twice</s>
<s>its easy to get lazy about edging which is really a twostep job  a horizontal cut anywhere the mower cant go then a vertical one to slice a line wherever the lawn meets beds and walks</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>trouble is this eats up trimmer string</s>
<s>so i attach extra precut pieces to the wand with velcro tape</s>
<s>i can pop in replacements quickly and im back inside for lunch</s>
</p>
