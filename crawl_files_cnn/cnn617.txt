<p>
<s>five years from retiring</s>
<s>think about where youd like to live in retirement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york money magazine  this story is part of money magazines retirement guide 2013 where youll find strategies to guide you through the last stretch starting at 10 years out then five years one year and finally your first year of leisure</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>with just five years left before you retire you need to begin solidifying your plans</s>
<s>make sure youre still on track but also nail down where youll live the largest expense in retirement and how youll meet your health care needs the second biggest</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>what to do</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>see if you need a course correction</s>
<s>a lot can happen between years 10 and five that could necessitate a change in plans  including an illness a job loss or an extended bear market</s>
<s>or maybe you just havent managed to accrue the ninetimessalary savings that would be ideal at this point</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>youre not out of luck you may be able to retire on less than youd hoped or get to your goal by working longer</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a onetime review with a financial planner who charges by the hour find one at napfaorg can be worth the $1000 or so investment to help you figure out where you stand</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>poll how much do you need to retire today</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>at a minimum plug your info into t rowe prices retirement income calculator trowepricecom to see your chances of retiring with your desired income</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>examine health care costs</s>
<s>better include an estimate for health insurance and outofpocket care costs in your incomeneeds projections</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>if youll retire before medicare kicks in at 65 you could have a big expense ahead</s>
<s>for a 62yearold couple with one spouse in ill health premiums run up to $2300 a month on the individual market according to ehealthinsurancecom</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>exchanges created under the 2010 reforms may reduce costs but youll still pay more than you do now</s>
<s>have an independent agent find one at nahuorg price plans for you</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related wheres your dream retirement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>you may also have the option to buy into your work plan for 18 months through cobra ask hr what that will cost</s>
<s>cobra may be the better option says cheryl fishparcham a policy expert at families usa</s>
<s>its not priced by age and has no exclusions</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>keep in mind medicare isnt a free ride either premiums and outofpocket costs average $4600 a year aarp reports</s>
<s>approximate your outlay at medicaregov</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>plan for ltc  asap</s>
<s>with a year in a nursing home topping $78000 longtermcare costs can drain your savings faster than a bear market says wealth planner jeff townsend</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>insurance can offset costs but premiums are as much as $4000 a year  and rising  for a couple in their early sixties</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>so buy only if your assets total $250000 to $15 million says san diego financial planner gil armour</s>
<s>below that youre likely to exhaust your money fast enough to qualify for medicaid above it you can pay your own way</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>if you are going to buy do so now because the older you are the higher the premiums</s>
<s>go with an insurer that does a lot of its business in ltc and gets an a or a b credit rating</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>make plans to get out of the house</s>
<s>now that your kids are grown and your work life is winding down do you really need that big home in the good school district</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a brookings institution paper found that housing near a highscoring public school costs an average of 24 times more than housing near a lowscoring one</s>
<s>so simply moving to a town nearby could slash your costs and free up cash for your nest egg</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related how far will my salary go in another city</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>downsize and youll bank even more</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>have another locale in mind</s>
<s>find out what it will cost to live there down to all the taxes says chicago planner cicily maton you might notice real estate taxes are lower but that could be offset by higher income or sales taxes</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>practice your retirement job</s>
<s>begin laying the foundation for any work youll do in retirement</s>
<s>ease the transition by getting credentials you need now or using relevant volunteer work to reorient your résumé</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>want to consult</s>
<s>get launched before you lose contacts in the field or your experience starts to look stale says art koff of retiredbrainscom</s>
<s>start picking up assignments on the side so that youll have steady clients at the ready when youre about to leave your job</s>
</p>
