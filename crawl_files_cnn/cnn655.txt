<p>
<s>a new adp report gives better insight into the health of the job market</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  private sector hiring jumped in october according to a report released thursday by payroll processor adp</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>private employers added 158000 jobs in the month adp adp fortune 500 said beating economists forecasts of 143000</s>
<s>the gains also topped septembers figures which were revised to 114000 from 162000 originally</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the october report is the first to feature adps new methodology aimed at further aligning its figures with the final monthly data released by the bureau of labor statistics</s>
<s>the adp report comes out a day before the governments official monthly jobs report though it has not always been a great predictor of what the bls report will say</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the revised adp report done in collaboration with moodys analytics now offers data on five categories of company size ranging from small businesses with no more than 19 workers to large companies with at least 1000 employees</s>
<s>it also breaks down employment in five industries  construction financial activities manufacturing professional and business services and trade transportation and utilities  rather than three</s>
<s>also the sample size has been increased to 406000 companies from 344000</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the largest employers are doing most of the hiring accounting for 69000 of the positions added in october said mark zandi chief economist at moodys analytics</s>
<s>they are 15 of the job base but have accounted for 25 of the job growth since the employment recovery began in early 2010</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related 100 best jobs in america</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>on the flip side the smallest employers are hiring at a much slower rate adding 18000 jobs in the month</s>
<s>these businesses with 19 or fewer employees make up 25 of the job base but only 12 to 13 of the growth</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>it feels like the job market is holding its own zandi said</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the professional and business services sector added the most jobs followed by trade transportation and utilities</s>
<s>a good sign for the economy zandi said is that 23000 construction jobs were created in october</s>
<s>its yet another indication that the housing market is bouncing back and zandi expects the gains to continue into next year</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>retailers are also hiring in anticipation of a decent holiday season</s>
<s>and the healthcare and leisure and hospitality sectors are also growing</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>manufacturing however lost 8000 jobs likely as a result of troubles in the european economy he said</s>
<s>and information services also shed positions</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related its not just a lowwage recovery</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the governments report is a more expansive look at the national employment picture since it includes both public and private sector hiring</s>
<s>the former has been shedding jobs for the past two years</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>both reports take on enhanced importance this month since they provide the last look into the health of the job market before the presidential election</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the national unemployment rate stands at 78 the lowest in nearly three years and the same rate as it was when president obama took office</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>going forward however companies are unlikely to boost their payrolls until the federal government resolves its fiscal issues zandi said</s>
<s>and the unemployment rate wont drop much until companies resume hiring</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>businesses are not confident enough to hire aggressively he said noting that the low rate of new business formation is also a drag on job growth</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>meanwhile about 363000 people filed for firsttime unemployment benefits in the week ended october 27 down 9000 from the previous week the labor department said thursday</s>
<s>that beat economists estimates of 375000</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>about 33 million americans continued to file for their second week of unemployment benefits in the week ended october 20 the most recent data available</s>
</p>
