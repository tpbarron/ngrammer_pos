<s>what to do</s>

<p>
<s>now dial back on stocks</s>
<s>at this point youll want to start shifting away from equities as the combined hit of withdrawals and a market downturn could compromise the longevity of your portfolio says baltimore financial planner crystal alfordcooper</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the max you should have in stocks 40 to 50</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>stresstest your spending</s>
<s>youre likely to have a sense now of your fixed expenses for the next few years</s>
<s>so outline a spending plan then measure it against your nest egg to make sure you wont blow through your savings</s>

<p>
<s>as a rule you can withdraw up to 4 of savings the first year then adjust by inflation in subsequent years and have a good shot at your money lasting 30 years</s>
<s>inventory your assets and see whether 4  along with social security pensions and parttime work  will cover your expenses</s>
<s>if not youll need to trim costs</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>whatever budget you decide on practice living on it now</s>
<s>youll realitycheck your plan while theres room for error</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>shore up your income</s>
<s>after health care costs retirees biggest fear is running out of money aarp found</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>one solution a lifetime immediate annuity</s>
<s>basically you pony up a chunk of money in exchange for a monthly check for life</s>
<s>a 65yearold man who invested $100000 today would get about $550 a month</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>you cant access money you put in however so invest only enough to cover the shortfall in basic living costs left after other guaranteed income</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>also dont go all in right away since interest rates partly determine payouts</s>
<s>invest in stages and youll benefit if rates rise</s>
<s>shop at immediateannuitiescom</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>pick up the pension check</s>
<s>lucky enough to have a traditional pension</s>
<s>find out what choices you have and how they affect your benefit</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>more than half of private industry workers with pensions can now opt to take their payout as a lump sum up from 23 15 years ago reports the labor department</s>
<s>dont bite</s>
<s>sure you can invest the money but itd be tough to generate the same income the annuity version guarantees over a long retirement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>related couple plans for the loss of a pension</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>another decision point with the monthly check youll have to choose whether to elect a survivor option that reduces your payouts but provides a 100 75 or 50 benefit to your spouse should you die first</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>you dont get a second chance on this decision says new york elderlaw attorney annmargaret carrozza</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>so project what would happen to your partners income without it</s>
<s>also investigate whether youd be better off taking the full benefit and using a portion to buy life insurance</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>stockpile cash</s>
<s>people tend to enter retirement with most of their money tied up in investments</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>bad idea says wealth planner tim golas</s>
<s>he suggests having enough cash to fund the first 12 months of living expenses separate from your emergency fund</s>
<s>this creates a safety valve so youre not at the whims of the market he says</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>start funneling money into a savings account cutting your 401k contribution to just capture the company match if needed</s>
<s>as a backstop move a portion of an ira into a shortterm bond fund</s>
