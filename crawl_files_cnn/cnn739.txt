<p>
<s>to solve the eternal quest for the perfectfitting denim a company called bodymetrics uses some innovative bodyscanning technology to help shoppers find the ideal fit without even trying the pants on</s>

<p>
<s>the body is the last piece of information to go digital bodymetrics ceo and founder suran goonatilake said</s>
<s>we use technology to enable a new retail format and we believe this is the next generation of apparel ecommerce</s>

<p>
<s>bodymetrics has been matching london shoppers with their jeans since the companys 2003 launch</s>
<s>later this year bodymetrics will be making its us debut  not in fashionsavvy new york or trendy los angeles but in silicon valley the heart of the tech world</s>
<s>customers of the bloomingdales in palo alto calif will soon get to try bodymetrics tech out for size</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>how does it work</s>
<s>customers enter the pod which is essentially a circular dressing room where 16 microsoft msft fortune 500 kinect devices scan the shoppers body clad only in underwear or tight gym clothes</s>
<s>in five seconds the kinects take more than 100 different measurements to make a 3d rendering of the buyers shape and size</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>as for privacy issues</s>
<s>its not really a bodyscan like you might be asked to do at an airport</s>
<s>the voluntary measurement simply maps the shoppers body shape by essentially making an outline with kinect sensors</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>once customers get their precise measurements they can go off and find the exact right pair of jeans for their body type</s>
<s>the bloomingdales in century city calif ran a pilot test in march for its denim days promotion</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>bloomingdales is always looking for new ways to excite our customers a department store spokeswoman said</s>
<s>merging technology with fashion by partnering with bodymetrics brings a new experience to our shoppers that brings ease to shopping</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>bodymetrics goonatilake says the technology isnt just for instore purchases either</s>
<s>later this year the company says it will make it possible for shoppers to scan their bodies from the comfort of their own homes as the software will be available for kinect users</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>that could help solve the well known problem of finding the right fit online</s>
<s>a number of fashion designers participate in whats known as vanity sizing with actual sizes differing widely from store to store</s>
<s>since there are no set standards for what constitutes a size small 8 or xxl are essentially meaningless</s>
</p>

<p>

<p>

<p>

<p>
