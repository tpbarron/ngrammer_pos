<p>

</p>

<p>

</p>

<p>
<s>mileage figures on most labels will be lowered by one or two miles per gallon according to the epa</s>
<s>the discrepancies involve about 900000 hyundai and kia vehicles already on the road</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the largest drop will be for the kia soul</s>
<s>its highway fuel economy estimate will be lowered by six miles per gallon taking it down to 29 mpg</s>
<s>two of the automakers most popular models the hyundai sonata and kia optima midsize sedans are not involved</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>fuel economy for the hyundai elantra a compact model that was named car of the year at last years detroit auto show will be reduced by one mile per gallon</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the fuel economy figures shown on automobile window stickers are usually based on testing done by the automakers themselves according to a strict laboratory testing procedure dictated by the epa</s>
<s>the epa then tests about 15 of all models itself to ensure automakers are performing the tests properly and reporting accurate results</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>while those audits occasionally result in fuel economy adjustments for a vehicle this is the first time any automaker has shown such a consistent overstatement in its mileage results according to the epa</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>hyundai and kia say the problem was an error involving a particularly complex part of the testing procedure</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>hyundai and kia are owned by the same south korean parent company hyundai motor group</s>
<s>the two brands operate independently in the united states but fuel testing is done jointly at a laboratory in korea</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>to make up the problem the automakers have agreed to issue debit cards to owners of models that were sold with overstated mileage figures</s>
<s>vehicle owners will be able to take their car or suv to a dealership to have the odometer checked and the number of miles driven recorded</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>owners will then receive a debit card based on the miles driven and the average local price of gasoline plus an additional 15</s>
<s>owners can have their odometers checked and get a debit as often as they like as long as they own the car</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>owners who have already sold their hyundai or kia vehicle will be able to receive a single debit card based on the mileage recorded on the cars bill of sale</s>
