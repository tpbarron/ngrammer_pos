<p>
<s>yahoo is paying lavishly for marissa mayer its fourth ceo in as many years</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>new york cnnmoney  yahoo coughed up a lot of cash to land its hotshot new ceo</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>marissa mayers compensation package could be worth an eyepopping $71 million over the next five years according to regulatory documents yahoo filed on thursday  and it would top $120 million if an extra batch of stock grants that she is eligible for comes through</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>mayers pay package is by far the most lavish that the company has offered to its recent string of ceos</s>
<s>on top of a $1 million annual salary and $2 million target bonus the deal includes a rich combination of stock grants and options</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>mayer is getting a stock grant valued at $14 million to compensate her for the google pay she left behind when she resigned from her previous employer on monday</s>
<s>those shares will vest in stages through 2014</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>yahoo also gave mayer a onetime $30 million retention award that will vest gradually over the next five years</s>
<s>half of the award will be a straight stock grant the other half will be stock options with performancebased vesting criteria</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>mayer also netted an equity award worth an additional $12 million in stock and options</s>
<s>that award will vest over the next three years</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>adding all of those equity awards together plus the $15 million in salary and bonus that she stands to collect over the next five years puts mayers total package at roughly $71 million  although its actual value will depend heavily on how yahoos stock fares and whether mayer hits her performance goals</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>her real payout could be significant higher</s>
<s>mayer will be eligible for additional equity grants each year which yahoo envisions will be not less than the $12 million she stands to collect for 2012</s>
<s>the size of those annual grants will be subject to the boards evaluation of mayers performance and current market compensatory levels and practices</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>yahoo has a history of being generous to its long string of incoming ceos</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>scott thompson who lasted a mere four months got a $1 million annual salary and incentives that could have pushed the value of his firstyear pay package as high as $26 million</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>instead thompson left with no severance after a scandal over his embellished educational credentials</s>
<s>he was able to keep around $7 million in cash and stock grants that yahoo yhoo fortune 500 had already made</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>yahoo also paid out $3 million last year in severance to carol bartz the ceo whom yahoos board fired in september</s>
<s>bartzs deal included accelerated vesting on stock grants and options that are worth millions more</s>
</p>

<s>as one of the search giants first employees she scored big when the company went public and has a net worth that is frequently estimated to be in the $300 million range</s>
