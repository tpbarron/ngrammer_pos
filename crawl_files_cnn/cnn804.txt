<p>

<s>geltech launched with $35 million in seed capital most of it from one angel investor</s>

<p>
<s>in march the us forestry service added fireice to its official qualified products list a stamp of approval that put the substance in front of federal firefighting agencies the military and municipal agencies</s>

<p>

<p>
