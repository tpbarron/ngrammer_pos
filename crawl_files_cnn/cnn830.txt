<p>
<s>fixed annuities are essentially cdlike investments issued by insurance companies</s>
<s>like cds they pay guaranteed rates of interest in many cases higher than bank cds</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>fixed annuities can be deferred or immediate</s>
<s>the deferred variety accumulate regular rates of interest and the immediate kind make fixed payments  determined by your age and size of your annuity  during retirement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the convenience and predictability of a set payout makes a fixed annuity a popular option for retirees who want a known income stream to supplement their other retirement income</s>
</p>
