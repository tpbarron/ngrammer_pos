<p>
<s>an annuity is an insurance product that pays out income and can be used as part of a retirement strategy</s>
<s>annuities are a popular choice for investors who want to receive a steady income stream in retirement</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>heres how an annuity works you make an investment in the annuity and it then makes payments to you on a future date or series of dates</s>
<s>the income you receive from an annuity can be doled out monthly quarterly annually or even in a lump sum payment</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the size of your payments are determined by a variety of factors including the length of your payment period</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>you can opt to receive payments for the rest of your life or for a set number of years</s>
<s>how much you receive depends on whether you opt for a guaranteed payout fixed annuity or a payout stream determined by the performance of your annuitys underlying investments variable annuity</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>while annuities can be useful retirement planning tools they can also be a lousy investment choice for certain people because of their notoriously high expenses</s>
<s>financial planners and insurance salesmen will frequently try to steer seniors or other people in various stages toward retirement into annuities</s>
<s>anyone who considers an annuity should research it thoroughly first before deciding whether its an appropriate investment for someone in their situation</s>
</p>
