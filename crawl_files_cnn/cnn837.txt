<p>
<s>hsbc reported monday that it turned a profit of $5 billion in the third quarter a figure held down by the scandalplagued banks efforts to absorb a series of fines and penalties</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the londonbased firm set aside $800 million to pay fines that may result from the banks alleged links to drug cartels and terrorist groups</s>
<s>in the second quarter the bank set aside an initial $700 million for the same purpose</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in july a us congressional committee issued a report detailing a lack of oversight at hsbc which resulted in thousands of suspicious transactions with clients of dubious repute</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>among other issues the report noted that in 2007 and 2008 hsbcs mexico unit shipped $7 billion in cash to the banks us affiliate a volume of shipments that law enforcement officials said could reach that size only if they included illegal drug proceeds</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>hsbc mexico had a number of highprofile clients linked to drug trafficking the report said as well as a huge backlog of accounts marked for closure due to suspicious activity but whose closures were delayed</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the report also found that hsbc worked extensively with saudi arabias al rajhi bank some owners of which have been linked to terrorism financing according to a cia report quoted by the subcommittee</s>
<s>some evidence suggests al rajhis key founder was an early financial benefactor of al qaeda the report said</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>even with $15 billion set aside to pay for its alleged indiscretions more may be needed</s>
<s>in a statement released monday chief executive stuart gulliver said that us authorities have substantial discretion on how to resolve the charges and that no agreement has been reached</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>indeed gulliver said the final amount of the financial penalties could be higher possibly significantly higher than the amount accrued</s>
<s> after the reports release the bank issued a statement acknowledging it had sometimes failed to meet the standards that regulators and customers expect</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>hsbc also earmarked $353 million to compensate customers sold payment protection insurance bringing the banks total hit for the quarter to $115 billion</s>
<s>the bank has been under fire for misleading clients regarding the purchase of certain kinds of insurance that prevented most policyholders from filing a claim</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in addition the bank is being scrutinized for its alleged role in the libor raterigging scandal</s>
<s>hsbc was subpoenaed in august by new yorks attorney general</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>barclays the first bank to settle with regulators paid uk and us regulators $453 million in penalties for rigging libor</s>
<s>the banks ceo resigned in the wake of that announcement</s>
</p>
