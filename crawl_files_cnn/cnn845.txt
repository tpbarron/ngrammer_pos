<p>
<s>immediate annuities start paying out right away and can prevent retirees from outliving their nest egg</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>plus with a fixed immediate annuity you lock in an income stream for your entire life or a specific number of years if you choose</s>
<s>knowing you can count on a guaranteed income stream can be a boon for retirees who dont want to worry if they will have enough money to pay the bills each month</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>plus the payments can be higher than you would normally be able to get by putting your money into safe investments on your own</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>with a variable immediate annuity you have the potential of keeping the buying power of your lifetime payments ahead of inflation by divvying up your investment among a variety of mutualfundlike portfolios</s>
</p>
