<p>

<p>
<s>after decades of decline cycling is booming</s>
<s>according to the department for transport people cycled farther in 2010 than in any year since 1991</s>
<s>in london some 540000 trips a day are made by bicycle—twice as many as in 2000</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>public transport is dearer and more crowded than it was and household incomes more pinched</s>
<s>and sir chris hoy who took three gold medals in cycling at the beijing olympics has raised cyclings profile</s>
<s>that the velodrome is among the most impressive of the new olympic venues in london is testament to the effect</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>public investment has helped too</s>
<s>boris johnson londons mayor and a keen cyclist has laid on jazzedup “cycle superhighways” that crisscross popular commutes</s>
<s>his most visible imprint on the city so far is a cyclehire scheme which has expanded eastwards</s>
<s>by 2026 mr johnson wants cycling to have quintupled from 2001 levels</s>

<p>
<s>to get there though cyclists will have to start looking less like mr johnson</s>

<p>
<s>nationally the richest 20 of people cycle an average of 33 miles a year to work compared with just nine miles for the poorest 20</s>

<p>
