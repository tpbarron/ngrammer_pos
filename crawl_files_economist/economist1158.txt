<p>
<s>how can italy get its act together</s>
<s>politicians have offered plans europe has issued warnings and delivered advice</s>
<s>but planning warning and advising are not enough</s>
<s>after 18 years of silvio berlusconi only dreaming will do</s>
<s>here is a costfree wishlist for 2012</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>1 get a new prime minister</s>
<s>if its a family manwoman who goes to bed at 11pm great</s>
<s>if heshe has a lover make sure theres only one at a time and that the lover isnt parachuted into parliament</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>2 get a new electoral law</s>
<s>the current one whereby party leaders can stuff parliament with their lawyers doctors clients relatives and lovers is a disgrace</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>3 get a new tax system</s>
<s>its absurd that only 017 of taxpayers 76000 people declare an income over e200000  $272000 when about 210000 luxury cars are sold in italy every year</s>
<s>make electronic payment the norm</s>
<s>no more mycreditcardmachineisontheblink routines in shops so they can get cash</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>4 get italian politicians to stay away from industry and business</s>
<s>sell off rai television enel electricity eni oil finmeccanica defence and aeronautics and bancoposta postoffice banking</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>5 scrap the current justice system</s>
<s>italian citizens—and foreign investors—cannot afford to be stuck in court for seven years waiting for a decision in a civil case</s>
<s>the current byzantine rules may keep lawyers happy but they make life impossible for everybody else</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>6 scrap upwards of 100 useless provincial authorities but keep municipalities and regions</s>
<s>thats more than enough</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>7 scrap the italian senate nowadays a useless duplicate of the lower chamber</s>
<s>transform it into the assembly of the italian regions 100 members no more</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>8 introduce a threeterm limit</s>
<s>some italian parliamentarians have been there for longer than most of the furniture</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>9 introduce a new system to reach markets and audiences outside italy</s>
<s>forget enit the national tourism board drop ice trade promotion and revamp the iccs italian cultural institutes</s>
<s>give italian embassies resources to promote italian interests abroad</s>
<s>italy makes stuff the world wants food fashion design but doesnt sell it effectively</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>10 introduce a “dostoyevsky rule” of crime and punishment into public life</s>
<s>too many characters are caught but get away with a reprimand or nothing at all</s>
</p>
