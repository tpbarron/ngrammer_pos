<p>
<s>winning an election tries a candidate’s mettle</s>
<s>but winning a place on the ballot paper is another kind of test</s>
<s>arcane and dubious eligibility criteria feature in many countries’ constitutions and electoral rules</s>
<s>the newly pluralist countries of north africa for example display what many might find a xenophobic streak</s>
<s>draft constitutions in egypt and libya require presidential candidates to ditch not only any foreign passports but also foreign spouses</s>
<s>tunisia is even harsher it disqualifies anyone with a foreign parent</s>
<s>even the keenest candidate can do little to shed that taint</s>
<s>a draft in egypt had a similar rule</s>
<s>but when it turned out that a leading salafist’s late mother had once held an american passport the wording changed</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>countries that have shed communist dictatorship are more liberal</s>
<s>estonia’s president toomas hendrick ilves was born in sweden of émigré parents</s>
<s>for unelected heads of state the regime is positively anarchic</s>
<s>most european monarchs have foreign forebears british aristocrats some claiming norman ancestry disparage the royal family’s german roots</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the young and ambitious may face a wait</s>
<s>most countries including the united states set a minimum age for the head of state at 35</s>
<s>france though dropped its threshold from 23 to 18 in 2011 whereas italy excludes the under50s</s>
<s>tajikistan is a rare example of an upper age limit you cannot be over 65</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>another hurdle is money</s>
<s>somalia’s new constitution published last month requires applicants to pay $10000 to register their candidacy</s>
<s>in liberia they must own property worth at least $25000</s>
<s>argentinian presidential candidates need a minimum annual income of 2000 pesos about $420</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>education levels matter too</s>
<s>turkey insists its president hold a university degree whereas the philippines’ constitution only stipulates the ability “to read and write”</s>
<s>kyrgyz ukrainian or uzbek hopefuls must speak the state language fluently</s>
<s>sierra leone requires english</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the trickiest requirements are the fuzziest ones</s>
<s>iran requires “trustworthiness and piety” iraq stipulates “a good reputation”</s>
<s>in the contest to elect singapore’s head of state in 1999 and 2005 the presidential elections committee deemed only one candidate sr</s>
<s>nathan to be a “person of integrity” among other stringent criteria and hence fit to run</s>
</p>
