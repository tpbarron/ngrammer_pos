<p>
<s>few countries you might suppose need a brewing revolution less than the czech republic</s>
<s>czechs are famed for their skill both at brewing and imbibing</s>
<s>yet all is not well</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>after the velvet revolution of 1989 czech breweries shifted to private hands</s>
<s>global chains moved in bought local brands and with their marketing and distribution knowhow soon came to dominate the market</s>
<s>a handful of brands such as pilsner urquell staropramen krusovice and stateowned budvar crowded out the rest</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>“i knew something was wrong when i moved to the country of beer and ten years later saw the same three beers everywhere” says max munson the american proprietor of jama a pub in prague</s>
<s>in 2009 and 2010 domestic beer sales contracted</s>
<s>bars started looking for variety to attract customers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in 2010 mr munson hooked up with pivovary lobkowicz a new firm that buys local breweries and helps them market and distribute their tipples better</s>
<s>lobkowicz now sells beer in 200 prague pubs and has seen sales grow by 13 and 72 in the past two years</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>craft beer is now booming</s>
<s>unfiltered unpasteurised or speciality beers brewed by the likes of bernard kocour svijany uneticky and zatec are available like never before</s>
<s>“it exploded” says max bahnson a beer writer who uses the pen name beer philosopher</s>
<s>overall domestic beer sales rose by 27  last year</s>
<s>euromonitor a marketresearch firm predicts solid growth through 2016</s>
<s>“sales have never been better” beams mr munson</s>
</p>
