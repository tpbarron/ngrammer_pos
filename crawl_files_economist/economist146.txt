<p>
<s>in the 1960s a french magazine editor françoise giroud recruited a crop of female political reporters to lexpress in hopes that they would charm their way to scoops</s>
<s>catherine nay still a working journalist recalls that she “turned up at the national assembly in a mini skirt and white maxi boots”</s>
<s>she ended up falling for a deputy who later became a minister—but she says nobody worried then about a conflict of interest</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>times have changed but ties between politics and media have not</s>
<s>three socialist ministers live with journalists</s>
<s>valérie de senneville wife of michel sapin the labour minister works for les echos</s>
<s>arnaud montebourg minister of “productive recovery” is the partner of audrey pulvar a broadcast journalist whose employer has said she can no longer conduct political interviews</s>
<s>vincent peillon education minister is married to nathalie bensahel of le nouvel observateur which is running a cover “les liaisons dangereuses women journalists and male politicians”</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>most arresting president françois hollande expartner of ségolène royal a former socialist presidential candidate is now companion to valérie trierweiler a journalist at parismatch and presenter of a cabletv show</s>
<s>this week the magazine decided to keep her as a journalist while mr hollande runs the country—though she will not cover politics</s>
<s>similar rules have been applied to others</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the french are not alone in mixing affairs of state and heart</s>
<s>gerhard schröder a former german chancellor met his fourth wife doris when she was a journalist</s>
<s>michael gove britains education minister is married to a journalist</s>
<s>but france seems more prone than others</s>
<s>dominique strausskahn disgraced former imf boss is married to anne sinclair a former tv presenter who is editor of the french huffington post</s>
<s>christine ockrent partner of bernard kouchner a former foreign minister was a tv news anchor</s>
<s>nor is it just on the left</s>
<s>alain juppé former foreign minister and jeanlouis borloo another exminister are married to journalists</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in a country that sets official store by equality nobody suggests ministerial spouses should stop work</s>
<s>carla brunisarkozy released an album while her husband nicolas sarkozy was president</s>
<s>indeed some newly downgraded journalists are indignant arguing that they are capable of separating professional and personal matters</s>
<s>on twitter ms pulvar mocked her demotion referring to “when i had a brain”</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>since the public interest stops at the bedroom door much in france is unspoken as jean quatremer says in his book “sex lies and the media”</s>
<s>for years the media kept quiet the existence of françois mitterrands outofwedlock daughter as well as the predatory behaviour of mr strausskahn</s>
<s>this is changing one poll finds that 54 think political journalists living with a politician should not stay in their job</s>
