<p>
<s>deep in the sweaty sierra leonean bush the land cruiser’s air conditioning broke down</s>
<s>no problem the carhire firm sent another car</s>
<s>dirty diesel from a diamondmining town fouled its engine and the second car broke down</s>
<s>again no problem</s>
<s>flash vehicle rentals sent a third toyota</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the small west african nation of sierra leone is not a good place to be stranded</s>
<s>it has few trustworthy places to get a car fixed</s>
<s>and it is not entirely safe</s>
<s>the civil war during which thousands of people had their hands hacked off as a punishment for voting ended in 2002</s>
<s>but your correspondent narrowly missed a riot in another mining town and the roads are always littered with cars wrecked by untrained drivers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>none of the big global carhire firms operates in sierra leone</s>
<s>yet the foreigners who have flocked here to invest in mines and farms need a reliable way to get around</s>
<s>david dobrowolski a 30yearold canadian seeks to supply one</s>
<s>with two friends he set up flash vehicle rentals two years ago</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>today its fleet comprises 70 fourwheeldrive toyotas which are good on terrible roads</s>
<s>some were bought at auctions—the aid agencies and un bodies in sierra leone typically sell their cars after five years or 100000km 62000 miles</s>
<s>most are simplified versions of the cars sold in the rich world with fewer temperamental electronics</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>sierra leone is though a tricky place to keep any vehicle going</s>
<s>there is no unleaded petrol available no toyota dealership and no recovery service</s>
<s>flash keeps its own store of spare parts and has imported a mechanic</s>
<s>it protects its highly portable assets with a gps system which tracks the location of each car and transmits it back to head office using the mobilephone network</s>
<s>if necessary flash can immobilise a car from afar</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>its chief selling point however is its breakdown service</s>
<s>it vows to send a replacement if any of its cars malfunctions anywhere in sierra leone</s>
<s>this gives customers peace of mind but it does not come cheap</s>
<s>within freetown the capital a flash car costs $110 a day excluding fuel</s>
<s>in the most remote patches of bush that can rise to $225 a day</s>
<s>some 90 of flash’s customers sign longterm leases with bespoke rates</s>
<s>shortterm hires cost more</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>mr dobrowolski who came to sierra leone in 2008 started flash because he was frustrated at the costly shoddy carhire services in freetown</s>
<s>it is a tough business—credit is expensive in sierra leone</s>
<s>but flash rushes in where avis fears to tread</s>
</p>
