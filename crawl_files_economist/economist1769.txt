<p>
<s>two years ago bill small toiled at one of new zealand’s largest wineries “pushing numbers around spreadsheets” and dreaming of striking out on his own</s>
<s>today he and his wife claudia make six wines including three sorts of sauvignon blanc under their own label</s>
<s>they sell them through naked wines a fouryearold company that claims to be britain’s fastestgrowing online wine seller</s>
<s>most wouldbe winemakers must flatter distributors schmooze potential investors and beg for socialmedia attention</s>
<s>“we don’t do that shoe leather” says mr small</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>like any online vendor naked wines bypasses middlemen and shares the savings with customers</s>
<s>but this is trickier for wines than it is for books</s>
<s>how do you convince oenophiles that they are buying fine vintages for plonk prices</s>
<s>especially when the same bottle is not for sale at the local offlicence</s>
<s>naked wines’ answer is to seek out gifted unknowns provide a stage and draw an audience</s>
<s>the idea is to create cachet without the slog of building brands</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>naked wines is not the only online company attempting this</s>
<s>new york’s warby parker claims to sell $600 spectacles for $95</s>
<s>san franciscobased everlane flogs smart clothes “with no crazy brand markups”</s>
<s>ning li a chineseborn entrepreneur started madecom after he realised that the £3000 $4 800 namebrand sofa he coveted was made in his hometown foshan for a tenth of the price</s>
<s>made promises “beautiful designs without the highstreet mark up”</s>
<s>naked wines is “a small part of something much bigger” says its boss rowan gormley “the end of people paying to be sold to”</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>that does not mean the end of salesmanship</s>
<s>to convince people to buy their stuff the newcomers must pull off a difficult trick exploit the upmarket streetcred that comes with a nologo attitude while forging brandlike bonds with consumers</s>
<s>naked wines’ website tells stories to try to reassure and inspire customers the recruitment of a famous label’s top winemaker the wooing of the first black south african woman to qualify as a winemaker</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>buyers hype the wines via comments and ratings “91 would buy again”</s>
<s>made which demands a bigger leap of faith sofas cost up to £1200 taps into the same sentiment by touting its designers and telling in mr li’s words “the story behind every product”</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the pitch is to richworld consumers who are unnerved by recession but still want fancy goods</s>
<s>it takes different forms</s>
<s>warby parker the name comes from two jack kerouac characters gives to someone who needs them one pair of glasses for each one it sells</s>
<s>naked has more than 100000 “angels” who pay £20 a month each in exchange for discounts</s>
<s>this provides the flow of cash needed to back naked’s upfront purchases</s>
<s>the firm usually pays for the grapes bottles corks and shipping rents winery space and even helps design the labels</s>
<s>the winemakers take little risk</s>
<s>they supply only their skills and are paid royalties</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>while scorning the hoopla of traditional brands the upstarts are racing to build brands of their own</s>
<s>everlane which is barely a year old has signed up 350000 “members”</s>
<s>after 2½ years of trading made has 80 employees and is breaking even</s>
<s>mr li is plotting a push into europe</s>
<s>sales of naked wines will jump 55 this year to £60m and the company will turn its first operating profit a year ahead of schedule says mr gormley</s>
<s>it too is expanding into america and australia</s>
<s>not all its customers are delighted</s>
<s>some find the marketing intrusive</s>
<s>one critic sniffed that she could only find one naked wine worth buying</s>
<s>others though seem happy to turn convention on its head—and get naked before they get drunk</s>
</p>
