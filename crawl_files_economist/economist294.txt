<p>
<s>lovers of thumping music and human sweat are growing scarcer</s>
<s>a third of nightclubs have shut in the past five years</s>
<s>the market value of the industry has fallen from £18 billion $36 billion to £14 billion since 2007 according to mintel a consultancy</s>
<s>the biggest nightclub firm luminar which owns oceana and liquid clubs went into administration last october</s>
<s>old hands saved it buying 66 of its 79 venues in december only 55 remain open</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>youngsters drink less often and frequently “predrink” when they do guzzling cheap supermarket booze before going out</s>
<s>the average customer now quaffs just 18 drinks at luminar’s clubs</s>
<s>a change in the licensing laws has also dented sales</s>
<s>before 2005 clubs had few rivals after 11pm when most pubs closed</s>
<s>they had “a licence to print money” admits aaron mellor of tokyo clubs</s>
<s>many pubs now offer alcohol later</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the economic downturn has made things worse</s>
<s>borrowing is trickier and jobless and cashstrapped youths are less likely to go out</s>
<s>universities which supply many clubbers have stopped expanding</s>
<s>a club with 100 fewer people than it can hold feels empty says jon collins head of cga strategy a nightclub consultancy—“and who wants to return to an empty club”</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>club owners have turned to social media to stave off decline</s>
<s>they recruit promoters—the young and popular mostly—to post links to their club nights</s>
<s>“guaranteed banger tonight” writes one</s>
<s>friends can add their names to a list for discounts</s>
<s>after a night out photos of revellers appear on friends’ facebook feeds branded with the club’s logo</s>
<s>peter marks head of luminar says 12m people view these pictures every month</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the best clubs now offer comedy live music and food in the day says michael oliver of mintel</s>
<s>but this is not an option for some</s>
<s>sound systems necessitate thick walls and few windows which does not make for an attractive dining space</s>
<s>mr marks says he gained little when his clubs offered comedy gigs and food</s>
<s>salvation may eventually come from a new floorfilling popmusic genre</s>
<s>but nobody can will that into being</s>
</p>
