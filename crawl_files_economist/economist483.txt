<p>
<s>chabad is a hebrew acronym for wisdom understanding and knowledge attributes of the divine upon which rabbi shneur zalman constructed his hasidic system of contemplation</s>
<s>his son and successor moved his “court” to the village of lubavitch</s>
<s>the seventh and last lubavitcher rebbe spiritual leader menachem mendel schneerson died childless in new york in 1994</s>
<s>many of his followers thought he was the messiah</s>
<s>the butmans still do</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the butmans’ colonialstyle home doubles as the phnom penh “chabad house”—a synagogue a restaurant a library a place where jews can come and socialise</s>
<s>there are chabad houses wherever jews live and as in phnom penh in places where they don’t live but might visit</s>
<s>in katmandu in nepal chabad annually hosts “the biggest seder on earth” for 2000 young jewish backpackers</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>in america almost every city and university campus has a chabad house</s>
<s>the movement has become a powerful force in jewish life</s>
<s>many people jews or gentiles who know little or nothing about judaism have bumped into a chabad emissary somewhere</s>

<s>but chabad is tolerant whereas the hallmark of haredism is intolerance towards nonobservant jews</s>

<p>
