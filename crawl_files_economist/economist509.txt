<p>
<s>greenlanders are assuming more powers of selfgovernment from denmark after the danes ceded control in 2009</s>
<s>in 2010 and beyond the government of the iceclad island will take control of domestic affairs</s>
<s>yet full independence remains elusive</s>
<s>that depends on being weaned off generous danish subsidies worth over $11000 a year for each of the 57000 or so greenlanders</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the government in nuuk the capital is bursting with ideas of how to get the economy running</s>
<s>one scheme is hydropower</s>
<s>the huge greenland ice cap some 3km nearly 2 miles deep in places may threaten the rest of the world as it melts and so raises the sea level but for locals it offers a bonanza torrents of meltwater to spin turbines</s>
<s>the government wants hydro to supply 80 or more of greenlands power</s>
<s>cheap and clean energy plus a cool climate could then lure investors</s>
<s>alcoa is considering whether to put an aluminium smelter in southern greenland</s>
<s>google or other internet firms running hot and energyhungry servers may be attracted to the big cool rock in the north atlantic</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>as greenlands ice retreats other economic activity will flourish</s>
<s>miners are prospecting newly revealed rock for gold rubies diamonds and more</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a bigger economic prize would be if longpromised deposits of oil and gas were found offshore</s>
<s>disappearing sea ice is making that task easier</s>
<s>expectations are high—oil has long been known to seep onshore</s>
<s>in 2007 the united states geological survey concluded that at least a few billion barrels of oil as well as trillions of cubic feet of gas are probably waiting to be found off greenlands east and west coasts</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the government is encouraging exploration 13 licences have been issued so far to large companies such as exxonmobil that are scouring over 137000 square kilometres 53000 square miles of the sea for the sweetest spot to drill</s>
<s>in 2008 the firms produced “very positive” seismic data from baffin bay says the government and exploration drills will be sunk in the coming year or so</s>
<s>the civil servant in charge of hydrocarbons in nuuk is confident that one will succeed</s>
<s>to make a discovery more likely soon another round of licensing will take place in 2010</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>striking a big deposit would cheer greenlanders and it could be helpful for the geopolitics of europe</s>
<s>europes increasing dependence on russia for energy makes it harder for politicians to stand up to threatening behaviour from the east</s>
<s>it would not hurt if an alternative supply of hydrocarbons were available from a friendly democratic country to the west</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>as for ties with denmark here is a longerterm prediction a declaration of full independence for greenland in 2021—the 300th anniversary of the date that is generally accepted to mark the beginning of danish colonial rule</s>
<s>oil riches naturally would lubricate the process</s>
</p>
