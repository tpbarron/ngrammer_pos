<p>
<s>telltale differences of style mark the campaign offices from which republicans urge mitt romney supporters to vote and those used by democratic volunteers for president barack obama</s>
<s>in the swing state of virginia the republican state headquarters fuels its troops on pizza a television is set to fox news and volunteers make calls on internet telephone terminals with buttons to record voters’ replies zapping the information to a central database</s>
<s>the mood is brisk and upbeat</s>
<s>“people are very energised” says christine o’connor a volunteer</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a few miles away in fairfax county a democratic bastion the mood is more emotional</s>
<s>a senior white house aide to mr obama valerie jarrett is visiting a field office in a personal capacity note staffers</s>
<s>tucked away in a shopping centre it is one of more than 60 obama offices in virginia—the republicans have only half as many</s>
<s>mrs jarrett offers reassurance and a sprinkling of obama stardust</s>
<s>“we really need him but the world needs him too” she tells one young man</s>
<s>handpainted posters bear such slogans as “keep va blue” and “sushi 4 obama”</s>
<s>a sign seeks donations of “healthy snacks”</s>
<s>calls are made on mobile phones with data entered into computers later</s>
<s>there is an emphasis on the local</s>
<s>on this particular evening the focus is on assigning volunteers who will work from “staging locations” in volunteers’ homes in a final fourday push</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>beyond style a difference of strategy divides the campaigns</s>
<s>a recent tightening of the race involved a surge among republicanleaning independents who are more certain to vote and are following the contest more closely than their democratic equivalents polls suggest</s>
<s>the romney ground game involves building on that momentum and widening that intensity gap</s>
<s>in contrast the obama plan is about mobilisation and adding democraticleaning voters hispanics blacks and young people to the pool of those who will actually cast ballots</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>a week before election day some 18m americans had voted already</s>
<s>mr obama has emphasised early voting allowed in many states as a way for poorer democrats to fit voting into busy lives</s>
<s>the president broke with tradition to vote in chicago on october 25th</s>
<s>the idea was to open the eyes of “sporadic voters” who might not find themselves free to vote on election day says mrs jarrett</s>
<s>in short democrats are betting that mobilisation will trump momentum</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>each side offers evidence that its ground game is winning</s>
<s>the obama camp points to leads among early voters in iowa or nevada</s>
<s>they note that in north carolina turnout is up among young voters and blacks</s>
<s>a week from election day in vital ohio voters from precincts the president won in 2008 had cast 53 of early ballots while 47 came from precincts that supported mr mccain</s>
<s>republicans cite swing states where the percentage of absentee and early voting by republicans is greater than the percentage of voters who are registered republican and where democrats are below trend claiming that as a swing</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>the obama camp points to its advantage in fieldoffice numbers more than 800 nationwide or more than twice the republican number</s>
<s>“buildings don’t make voter contacts” retorts a virginia republican boasting of gaining 1000 new volunteers in 48 hours after mr romney won the first presidential debate</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>obama party workers say that months of canvassing have left them with finelycrafted lists of likely supporters</s>
<s>an obama volunteer in fairfax bob polkinghorn marvels that the voters on his call lists have already been contacted by the campaign at least once and sometimes three times</s>
<s>in 2008 republicans were “clobbered” technologically by team obama concedes a republican party boss</s>

<s>the virginia party boasts of “moms for mitt” phone banks and “warrior wednesdays” when exservicemen call each other</s>
<s>both sides claim targetvoter lists of equal “efficiency” the romney camp reports that 63 of those they contact are supporters avoiding wasted time and resources</s>
<s>the obama camp reports 65 efficiency in voter contacts—a measure on which democrats enjoyed a hefty lead in 2008</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>strip away the competing claims and early voting data essentially tally with the picture of the race painted by statebystate polls says michael mcdonald an electoral expert at george mason university</s>
<s>both parties have become more skilled at mobilising voters in the past decade in part by returning to tactics familiar to politicians of the 1800s he adds</s>

<p>
<s>the parties have both embraced research showing that nothing beats a personal appeal from “trusted peers”—friends neighbours relations or those with shared interests—either on a doorstep or by telephone</s>

<p>

<s>on average romney backers are whiter older more affluent and have moved around less than the general population all that fits profiles of those most likely to vote</s>
