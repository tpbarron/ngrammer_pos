<p>
<s>in 2010 boris johnson will give london cyclists something to smile about</s>
<s>the mayor plans to launch a bicyclehire system modelled on similar ones in paris barcelona and a growing number of other cities around the world</s>
<s>with some 6000 bikes and 400 docking stations the scheme at first covering about 17 square miles 44 square kilometres of central london should allow quick and relatively cheap access to rental bikes</s>

<p>
<s>there will be difficulties to overcome</s>
<s>securing land for bike stations in the busiest parts of london will need strong collaboration between transport for london tfl which is commissioning the scheme the royal parks and the nine boroughs involved</s>
<s>then theres the cost £140m $229m over six years</s>
<s>the aim is that over time the project will pay for itself</s>
</p>

<p>

<p>
<s>a bigger worry may be safety</s>
<s>the bikes will encourage large numbers of new tentative cyclists to ride—or wobble—onto some of londons busiest roads</s>
<s>the scheme is expected to generate an extra 40000 journeys a day</s>

<s>plans to have 12 “cycle superhighways” by the end of 2012 should help eventually</s>

</p>

<p>
