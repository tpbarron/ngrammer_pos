<p>
<s>the best time to come before a judge is never</s>
<s>but courtroom lore suggests that after lunch is better than before it</s>
<s>a study of israeli parole hearings shows that just before a meal the chances of success are slim</s>
<s>but other biases abound too</s>
<s>a study of 181 american trial judges this summer in science a journal showed that explaining the biology of psychopathy brought lighter sentences by an average of one year</s>
<s>when the defence simply told the judges that the convict was a psychopath the average sentence was 14 years</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>judges—flawed decision makers like all humans—find sentencing the hardest bit of their job</s>
<s>paul chernoff a retired american judge says determining guilt or innocence is easier</s>
<s>biases are visible across countries and cultures for the same offence male culprits get harsher sentences than women</s>
<s>black criminals serve longer sentences than white ones</s>
<s>attractive women and babyfaced men get shorter sentences</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>seemingly peripheral factors can skew decisions too</s>
<s>in a 2006 german study judges were asked to roll rigged dice between reading the documents in a hypothetical case and making their judgment</s>
<s>those who rolled a one gave lower sentences than those who rolled a six</s>
<s>a response to such studies has been the introduction of mandatory guidelines for judges in america</s>
<s>they recommend what sentence to give for which crime based on previous cases</s>
<s>judges dislike having their hands tied down in such a way</s>
<s>this week a freedomofinformation request showed 22 judges in ohio are trying to have such rules relaxed</s>
</p>

<p>
<s>other perhaps better remedies are on offer too</s>
<s>one is training</s>
<s>birte englich a psychologist at the university of cologne suggests exposing judges to the occurrence of biases and playing games that make them less susceptible</s>
<s>another would be to use several judges in criminal cases</s>
<s>for instance in germany three judges decide on crimes carrying a maximum sentence of less than three years for more serious crimes five judges are required</s>
</p>

<s>in new south wales australia the law reform commission wants to increase public involvement in sentencing</s>
